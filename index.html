The Legend & Legacy of the Nine-Eyed Dzi

Steeped in mystery, the Nine‑Eyed Dzi is revered as the “King of Dzi Beads,” a talisman believed to channel prosperity, power, and divine protection. Legends speak of its celestial descent – gifted by the gods, or crafted by the serpent kings of ancient lore. Its nine eyes represent the nine virtues: compassion, glory, eternal life, energy, fame, dignity, power, control, and the removal of obstacles.
