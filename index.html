Legend of the Nine-Eyed Dzi

The Nine-Eyed Dzi is one of the most revered Tibetan talismans, symbolizing power, protection, and prosperity. Ancient legends tell of these beads falling from the skies, emerging from sacred rivers, or being discovered in hidden caves. Each bead carries centuries of mystery and spiritual energy, believed to bring good fortune to its holder.

Symbolism & Power

Dzi beads have been cherished for their spiritual and energetic properties. In Tibetan culture, they are believed to act as protectors, amplifiers of intention, and vessels of positive energy. For centuries, they have been worn by monks, royalty, and adventurers seeking divine blessings.
